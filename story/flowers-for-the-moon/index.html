Flowers for the Moon

About the Author

Clio Yun-su Davis is a game designer and writer who splits her time between DC, New York City, and Austin, Texas. She studied interactive storytelling in the Interactive Telecommunications Program at NYU’s Tisch School of the Arts, a graduate program she likes to describe as “sci-fi Hogwarts.” When not writing fiction or creating games, she can usually be found working on an immersive theater project or living one of a thousand possible lives in a larp.
