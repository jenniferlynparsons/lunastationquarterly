About the Author

Chicana Feminist and former Rodeo Queen, Tisha Marie Reichle-Aguilera (she/her) writes so the desert landscape of her childhood can be heard as loudly as the urban chaos of her adulthood. She is obsessed with food. Her stories have been anthologized in Made in L.A. Her flash fiction has been included in Best Small Fictions 2022. A former high school teacher, she earned an MFA at Antioch University Los Angeles and is a PhD candidate at the University of Southern California. She is a Center Theater Group Writersâ€™ Workshop Participant, a Macondista, and works for literary equity through Women Who Submit.
