In 2025, after the first successful run of a Roman ring spun on cosmic string was recorded, the staff of Cal Tech were ecstatic. The madness after the travel period was not associated with any anatomical brain changes- histological studies were all normal. This paper is part two of a forty-five-segment study logging the effects of time movement on unprimed human subjects.
