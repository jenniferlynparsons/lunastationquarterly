Today feels like a much more bright, spring day than the actual launch day did for this issue. The sun is shining and I have the windows open for the first time in months. 

Speaking of launch days, yes, the issue came out last week! Did you miss it? That makes sense. I had a family emergency and so I didn't get to fanfare the release as much as usual. But I'm here now! And the issue is out! And I have some fun news about it!

Some of you may be aware that I run experiments. LSQ is never a static entity, always growing and changing to meet our readers where they are and support the staff and authors better. Some of these experiments don't pan out and that's okay because some of them do and that means every version of LSQ is the best version so far.

There are two experiments for this year. The first one is not quite ready yet, but will be soon (follow us for announcements!). Let's just say there will be some new benefits for Patreon supporters and the return of a much-requested old website feature. That's all I can share about that for now, but I can definitely talk about the second experiment.

I've got the current issue up in a _lot_ more places for you all! You can now get LSQ in most digital bookstores (still working on bookshop.org) and you can now buy print editions in more places as well as direct from us!!!
I'll be posting more details on how this helps support LSQ (including some financial transparency) soon, so you can make clear choices when supporting us. 

For now, I hope you enjoy the issue, wherever you choose to purchase it. Please check out the [issue page](https://www.lunastationquarterly.com/issues/065/) for all the details!
