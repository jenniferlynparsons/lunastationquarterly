Clio Yun-su Davis is a game designer and interactive media writer. After earning a master's degree from the Interactive Telecommunications Program at NYU's Tisch School of the Arts in 2015, she co-founded Janitor Interactive, an interactive media production company. Projects with Janitor Interactive include BATTLE OF THE BOY BANDS, a card game that was accepted into the Boston Festival of Indie Games Tabletop Showcase in 2016 and the PUPPET VR MURDER MYSTERY with director Erin Finnegan.
