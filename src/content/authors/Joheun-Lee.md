Joheun (Jo) Lee is a Korean-to-English literary translator. With roots in K-pop fandom and a love of subcultures, she is drawn to stories of desire, relationships, and identity by women and queer writers. Her translations include Lee Heejoo’s Holy Boy and Amil’s The Forest Called You (both forthcoming in 2026). She lives in Shanghai with her partner and four cats.
