Jalyn Renae Fiske is an author, editor, educator, and librarian. She earned her MFA in Creative Writing from Goddard College in 2017. That same year, she began her tenure as Fiction Editor for James Gunn's Ad Astra magazine and was the Editor-in-Chief for The Pitkin Review. Jalyn writes blends of science fiction, fantasy, and horror that are at times unsettling, strange, or whimsical. Her work has appeared in Allegory, Wyldblood, Mythaxis, Fabled Planet, Sword and Sorceries, and The Science Fiction Tarot anthology among others. In 2024, she published her award-winning debut short story collection, Side Quest: Stories. Find her at www.jrenaewriter.com.
