Sadie writes science fiction, fantasy, and horror. Some of her recent work can be found in Flash Phantoms, Corner Bar Magazine, Midnight Ink: 2025, and Altered Worlds: An Altered Reality Anthology. She has also been awarded a Silver Honorable Mention from the Writers of the Future Contest for the 4th Quarter of 2025. She is currently working on a science fiction novel.

When not writing or working her day job as a freelance web developer, she enjoys gardening, absurd bumper stickers, and befriending street cats.
