Pauline Barmby (she/her) is a Canadian astrophysicist who believes that you can’t have too many favorite galaxies. She lives in London, Canada and hopes to someday visit her namesake main belt asteroid, minor planet 281067. Her fiction has appeared in Utopia Science Fiction, Analog, and the Year’s Best Canadian Fantasy and Science Fiction series. Find out more at www.galacticwords.com.
