Greta Hayer received her MFA at the University of New Orleans and has fiction appearing in Beneath Ceaseless Skies and nonfiction published in Booth and Flint Hills Review. She received a bachelor's degree in history from the College of Wooster, where she studied fairy tales and medieval medicine. She lives in New Orleans with her husband and their three strange cats. Follow her Instagram @Greta_Hayer.
