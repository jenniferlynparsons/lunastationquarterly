M. McNamara is a Brisbane-based author who dabbles in fantasy, science fiction, and a smidge of horror. She has written three books and many short stories; her work has appeared in Of Metal and Magic, The Pink Hydra, and the Of Love & Dragons anthology by Flame Tree, among other publications. Her short story ‘Welcome to the Fantasy Fair’ is also a podcast on Connor Reads Books. If you like her work, please visit her website at mmcnamarabooks.com, or on Instagram @m_mcnamara_books.
