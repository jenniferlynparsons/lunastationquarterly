Ellen Parent is a writer and teacher from rural Vermont. Her YA apocalyptic novel, After the Fall, was released in 2024. Her poetry has been published in Vermont Magazine, Bloodroot, Birchsong Vol II, and elsewhere. She was a finalist for the Vermont Writer's Prize, a Nautilus Book Award silver medalist, and a finalist for DragonCon's Mike Resnick Memorial Award for Fiction. She also has two young kids and, coincidentally, could use a nap.
