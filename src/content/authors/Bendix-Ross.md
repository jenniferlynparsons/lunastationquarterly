Bendix Ross grew up near the ocean, often staring into the water with an uneasy sense of what might live beneath it. She writes speculative fiction intended to leave readers wondering. Ross’ work has appeared in Weirdscapes: An Otherworldly Anthology. The author lives in the United States’ Pacific Northwest and can be found on Bluesky@bendixross.bsky.social.
