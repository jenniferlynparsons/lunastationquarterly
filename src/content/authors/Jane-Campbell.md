Jane Campbell has an MFA from the University of British Columbia. Her work has appeared in publications including Hazlitt, Grain, EVENT, Show Me All Your Scars: True Stories of Living with Mental Illness, and Best Canadian Essays. She lives on the unceded ancestral territories of the Coast Salish peoples, Sḵwx̱wú7mesh (Squamish), Sel̓íl̓witulh (Tsleil-Waututh), and xʷməθkʷəy̓əm (Musqueam) Nations, in Vancouver, British Columbia with her family. She loves the ocean and is learning how to SCUBA dive.
