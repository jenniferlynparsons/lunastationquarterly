Sylvie Althoff (she/her) is a queer transgender woman who works as a writer, editor, elementary teacher, and jazz banjoist. Her speculative fiction has appeared on recommended reading lists by Locus and Reactor, and has been published in venues including Escape Pod, Small Wonders, Saros, and The Daily Tomorrow. She lives in Lawrence, Kansas with her wife, musician and teacher Jenn Thomas; their dog, Nomi Malone; and their layabout cat, Pocket. She may be found at sylviealthoff.com or on Bluesky @sylvie-althoff.
