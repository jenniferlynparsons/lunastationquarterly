Feby Idrus is a writer, musician and arts administrator from New Zealand. She has recently been published in Small World City, Takahē and Carmina Magazine. When not writing, she is teaching kids flute, organising concerts, and trying to find more time to read. She's 80% sure she hasn't seen a ghost.
