Summer Jewel Keown hails from the Midwest, where she experiences all the weather. She recently edited the fiction collection What Rides at Night: Queer, Feminist, Fantastical, Bicycle Halloween Stories, a Bikes in Space anthology from Microcosm Publishing. She has stories in other installments in the series, as well as Written with Pride and Pulp Literature. She has also published two sapphic romance novels as her alter-ego, Sofi Keren. Visit her at medusafish.com.
