Safiya Cherfi is a web developer who lives in Scotland with her husband, son, and two cats. She loves exploring the speculative and the historical through her writing. One of her manuscripts was long-listed for the 2021 Laxfield Literary Launch Prize and now competes for her attention amongst several other works in progress. She has short stories published in Gutter, The Selkie, Inner Worlds and more.
