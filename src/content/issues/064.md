We here at Luna Station Quarterly enjoy the standard holiday fare as much as anyone. That said, this issue has none of that whatsoever!

If you need a break from the season, and would in fact prefer aging druid veterinarians, adorable bee-cat hybrids, a vengeful weather forecaster who can control storms, nuclear faerie beetles, living nail art, an unemployed dragon rider and the usual cast of witches, selkies and the like, you've come to the right place.

Sneak this tome of wonders in your bag come holiday time, kids! It'll keep you busy during the interminably long hours of family strife. Or at the very least, it'll get you banned from the table. Either way, you'll thank us! See you next year!
